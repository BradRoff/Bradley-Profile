What is MHE?

     Multiple Hereditary Exostoses (MHE) is a rare bone disorder characterized by the growth of bumps on bones, known as osteochondromas, which can grow to extremely large sizes. For example, I have undergone multiple surgeries to remove these bumps, including one the size of a baseball from my leg. Despite lacking nerves, these growths can cause pain by impacting various structures within the body. Working with computers and technology has provided me with an escape from the medical challenges I faced. Fortunately, today I am in a medically stable condition and eager to expand my cybersecurity knowledge. If you are interested in learning more about MHE, I recommend visiting the MHE Research Foundation's website!"
